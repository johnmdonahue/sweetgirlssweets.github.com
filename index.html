Our Story

Sweet Girls' Sweets is my home-based business here in Lexington, KY started after many years of encouragement from family and friends. All desserts are prepared after the order is placed - not ahead of time - and most fruits are hand-picked or purchased from local Kentucky growers. All crusts are made from scratch and rolled by hand.

Freshness is our top priority!

The Name

The name's meaning is really two-fold. Most simply, I chose the name because sweet girls (and even not-so-sweet girls) love to eat sweets. Obvious enough, huh? Actually, the name has a very personal meaning. The girls in my family have been baking together and using these recipes for many generations. I was raised (and still enjoy) baking with my mother and grandmother. I now have two girls of my own and have the joy of teaching them how to bake. When naming my business, I thought of my past and looked to my future … when my girls will be preparing Sweet Girls' Sweets.

30 Years of Experience

I have been baking since before I can remember. My mother had me at her side in the kitchen from the time I was tall enough to reach the counter and stovetop while standing in a chair! I made my first pie when I was 3. It was a lime meringue and I remember it fondly. With my mother's guidance, I made the pie from crust to filling to meringue! I've been baking ever since!
